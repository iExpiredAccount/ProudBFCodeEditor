Soy Sauce Chicken (SD)

Today's breakfast dish draws on East Asian influences.
Soy Sauce Chicken broiled in ginger, garlic cloves, and spring onions.


Inspired By

Home-made soy sauce chicken that only took 30 minutes!

Ingredients of Note

  1. Spring onions cut sideways
  2. 6 ginger cloves
  3. garlic chunks

Personal Note

This dish was a canteen staple in secondary school.
I used to detest the taste of this because it was too vinegary, but it was brought to my attention several years later that
vinegar was as much a preservative as it was for taste.
